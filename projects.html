Into the abyss

Into the abyss is the latest psychological thriller experience from Re_Soul.

The story follows Cassandra Miller, a 17 year old girl that after a traumatic event, her life goes into a downward spiral. 2 years after the incident, devastated and terrified, Cassandra desperately decides to go back to where it all started, her own house, In her quest Cassandra will have to go to hell and back in order to find peace at last where she is willing to give her life to stay at peace.

Jungle Inferno

The first experience from Re_Soul, called “Jungle Inferno”. It tells a story about the Vietnam War.

As a nameless soldier that just joined the war, you must face the horrors that await deep in the jungle, you will be met with great obstacles, and enemies, only to find out that the greatest enemy lives at home and perhaps even inside of oneself.
